About us
amazing Nature

The air in Skyfall is so clean and fresh. But warm clothes will still come in handy, as it can be cold at altitude. This is the time for such exciting activities as extreme snowmobile rides, ice skating rink.
healthy place

Skyfall is considered a climatic resort, this is facilitated by the abundance of the sun, the mild climate, and the healing properties of the mountain air. Each vacationer who purchases a ticket to Dombai gets the opportunity not only to “get infected” with activity, but also to improve their health. Water from mountain lakes has a healing and rejuvenating effect, clean air treats lung diseases.
Something new

Today, the Skyfall ski resort continues to develop rapidly. By the beginning of the new season, it is planned to open a new ski slope, increase the number of rental points and catering places. Every year the system of organization of recreation is improved, the quality of work of service departments is improved.
